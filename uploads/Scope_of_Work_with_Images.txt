SCOPE OF WORK

**Project Overview**:
The selected vendor will be responsible for the design, development, and implementation of a comprehensive web-based application. The project includes the following key components:

1. **System Design**: 
   - Develop a detailed system design document based on requirements.
   - Ensure scalability and flexibility to accommodate future needs.

2. **Development and Customization**:
   - Build the application using the latest technologies.
   - Customize features to meet the specific needs of the institution.

3. **Integration**:
   - Integrate with existing systems such as ERP, HRMS, and financial systems.

![Workflow Diagram](A_detailed_and_professional_workflow_diagram,_show.png)

4. **Testing**:
   - Conduct thorough testing including unit tests, integration tests, and user acceptance tests.

5. **Deployment**:
   - Deploy the application on the institution's servers.
   - Provide training and support to users.

6. **Post-Implementation Support**:
   - Provide support for a minimum of 12 months after deployment.
   - Offer maintenance and enhancement services as needed.
